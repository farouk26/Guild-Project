What Are We?

We are a mixed bunch of mostly MMO Vet's and some Beginners that provide a mature and friendly Environment Mature and Friendly Environment for like minded Adventurers. Being an international Guild, every Member adds his/her own charm. Our Guilds credo is to enhance each others Black Desert Online experience with laughter, knowledge and positive Attitude. We dont say fancy words, just to attract other Players. Neither do we use Guildies concealad as new interested Forumspeople, to keep us in the Headlines

We take the Mature part serious and prefer Quality over Quantity. Therefore you wont see us skyrocketing to 100 Members straight away. Our Guildchat can also be silent at times, but thats because we have People taking care of Family, Work and School. So - fair Warning- if your expecting braindead chat action (traps are gay and so on) with immature people taking over, well then we wont be the guild for you

Our Events Are Player-driven

Every member can start an event in the game. The member becomes the organizer and is responsible for the activity. Other members can participate for the event by simply voting for yes, no or maybe. The organizer informs them on time and place to meet, and what to bring. Events can entail anything.In Moonguard you are in charge of – but therefore also responsible for – your own fun and gaming experience

Moonguard Weekly Weekend Events

To help our players out, we have a few preset events on Thursday and Friday evening, Saturday morning and Sunday afternoon. These include combat group content for high levels, sea monster hunting and blue whale hunting, guild bosses. They are announced by the guild officers or the guild leader either In-game or on Discord
