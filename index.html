Guild Introduction

Moonguard is an international gaming community with a strong preference for MMO’s and open world PvP. We aim to provide a fun, mature and well organised gaming environment for adults. In Moonguad we like to do things our own way. You will not find us racing to the highest level or eating through new content in a day or two. Instead we enjoy, explore and evolve in our own pace. We strive to be a non-hierarchical and easy going community where everybody is treated equal, fair and with kindness. And we even encourage our members to have a real life. If you are the type of player that plays the same MMO for years and doesn’t stray to other games, we are just the guild for you. If you are a game hopper, we definitely are not the community you are looking for.

Our population is in general older then common in games and on average between 16 and 30 years of age. We have members from many countries: so we are quite the international bunch. Our prime time is between 18.00 and 23.00 hours but you’ll find members online on most hours of the day.

Recruitement & Requirements

We are looking for the type gamer that embraces the guild as their virtual home, adopts his guild mates as new best friends and is motivated to stick around for a long time. To become a member you need to be a pureblood team player. You enjoy both banter and good conversations on our voice communication. We also expect you to be the proud owner of a decent English vocabulary that does not include excessive swearing and cursing. We have an age requirement of 18+. We don't have a level requirement and welcome both new and experienced players.

Events Calendar

In Moonguard you’ll find a pleasant mixture of activities. We are very proud of our events calendar which offers you daily events and a lot things to do and to enjoy in Black Desert with your guild mates. Take your pick from grinding groups in Mediah, Valencia or Pirate Island, node wars, guild versus guild, trading, whale hunting, gathering, fishing or boss scrolls. Each event allows you an quick and easy way to RSVP. And if you are new to an event, clicking on it offers you all the necessary information, guides, instant Discord connect, directions and even embedded video tutorials and a time zone converter. We make it easy for you to group up and enjoy company. All you need to do, is sign up and show up

Applications

If you feel it is about time you become Moonguarder too, please don’t hesitate to fill out the short application form on our website. If your application is approved, you will start your trial period of 14 days. During this time we will decide if you are a good match for us and you can decide if you enjoy our community. We decide this mainly based on your activity in voice chat

Discord Is Mandatory

We are using Discord (available for free for all devices) for voice and text chat and it is our main communication tool. We use it to share links, video's, to give notices of absence and ofcourse we use it for voice chat
